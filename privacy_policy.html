Privacy Policy for GKP Radio

Effective Date: April 9, 2026

Thank you for using the GKP Radio app. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding any information collected when you use our application.

1. Information Collection and Use

The GKP Radio app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, user information, or device information. Our application operates solely as a radio streaming client.

2. Third-Party Services

The app requires an internet connection to stream audio content. We do not integrate with third-party tracking services or ad networks that collect personal information.

3. Children's Privacy

Our app does not intentionally collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. Given that we do not collect personal data, no children's data is processed or stored.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
