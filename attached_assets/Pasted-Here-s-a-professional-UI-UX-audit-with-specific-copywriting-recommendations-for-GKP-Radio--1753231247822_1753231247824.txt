Here's a professional UI/UX audit with specific copywriting recommendations for GKP Radio:

### **Critical UI/UX Issues & Copywriting Flaws**
1. **Inconsistent Terminology**  
   - *Problem*: "Prayer Requests" becomes "Paper Requests" (Community Page) and "Frayer Requests" (Video Library).  
   - *Fix*: **Standardize all instances to "Prayer Requests"** for brand consistency.

2. **Offensive Typos**  
   - *Problem*: "North Korea" under Community section (Video Library footer) appears to be a catastrophic autocorrect error.  
   - *Fix*: **Replace with "Youth Values" or "Faith Journeys"** + implement QA process for copy.

3. **Meaningless Stats**  
   - *Problem*: Numbers without context (e.g., "2.430 | 1.19" under videos).  
   - *Fix*: **Add clear labels**: "2.4K views | 119 comments | 3 days ago"

4. **Broken Microcopy**  
   - Examples:  
     - "Received Me" → **"Join Live"** (Live Page buttons)  
     - "mail talk" → **"Real Talk"** (Youth Hour description)  
     - "worship meals" → **"worship melodies"** (Video description)

5. **Unprofessional CTAs**  
   - "Give your email" → **"Subscribe for Updates"**  
   - "Received Me" → **"Notify Me"** or **"Remind Me"**

6. **Incorrect Scripture References**  
   - Matthew 16:30 & 18:30 don't exist.  
   - **Correct to Matthew 18:20** universally.

---

### **Tone & Messaging Improvements**
1. **Community Headline**  
   Original: "Connect with believers worldwide..."  
   **Stronger**: *"Find Your Faith Family: Share Stories, Request Prayers & Grow Together"*

2. **Video Library Tagline**  
   Original: "Watch powerful sermons..."  
   **Engaging**: *"Fuel Your Faith: On-Demand Sermons, Healing Sessions & Youth Programs"*

3. **Prayer Request UX**  
   Original: "Please keep my mother in your prayers..."  
   **Action-Oriented**: *"Join Us in Prayer: My mother undergoes surgery this week. Your prayers strengthen our family."*

4. **Live Chat Headline**  
   Original: "Join our global community..."  
   **Urgent**: *"Worship Live Now: Global Prayer & Teaching in Progress"*

### **Brand Voice Guidelines**
1. **Core Principles**  
   - ✅ **Warmth**: Use "we/us" language (*"Our community will pray for you"*)  
   - ✅ **Hope**: Emphasize positive outcomes (*"Discover healing through prayer"*)  
   - ❌ **Avoid**: Robotic stats ("45K Prayers") → humanize (*"45,000 Prayers Lifted Up"*)

2. **Section Renaming**  
   | Current | Recommended | Reason |
   |---|---|----|
   | "Hailing" | **Healing Ministry** | Corrects typo + clarifies purpose |
   | "Earth Stories" | **Faith Testimonies** | Avoids confusion with environmental content |
   | "Pedestria" | **Podcasts** | Industry-standard terminology |

3. **Button Copy Hierarchy**  
   ```diff
   - [Join]
   + [Join Live Stream]
   
   - [Give your email]
   + [Get Daily Encouragement]
   ```
